About

  • Tottenham Hotspur Football Club is a professional football club located in London, England, that competes in the Premier League.
  • The club was founded in 1882 originally as Hotspur Football Club. It wasn't until 1884 that the club was renamed to the current name of Tottenham Hotspur Footbal Club.
  • Tottenham has a long-standing rivalry with fellow North London football club Arsenal.
  • Before the Premier League was founded in 1992, Tottenham was one of the five clubs pushing for its founding.
  • Nicknames for the club include "Spurs" and "the Lilywhites".
