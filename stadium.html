Tottenham Hotspur Stadium

Tottenham Hotspur Stadium was opened on 3 April 2019. It has a capacity of 62,303 people and is the largest football club stadium in London.
The stadium is designed like a concert hall with good acoustics in mind so as to optimise the atmosphere on match day.
